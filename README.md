# lcd-lab-abdallah
Abdallah Brahim-4/15/2026

LCD project for IoT lab 11
    calculates the player's reaction time with the help of a button and displays it on a LCD
Needs:
Arduino IDE, Arduino UNO R3
LCD display
Wiring LCD:
	RS to Digital Pin 7
	EN to Digital Pin 8
	D4 to Digital Pin 9
	D5 to Digital Pin 10
	D6 to Digital Pin 11
	D7 to Digital Pin 12
	VSS to GND
	VDD to 5V
	VO to 1kΩ resistor to GND
	RW to GND	A (Backlight +) to 5V
    K (Backlight -) to GND
